Premium Gift Wrapping

Signature gold-leaf wrapping paper with hand-tied silk ribbons. We can also include a wax-sealed handwritten note for that authentic touch.

Custom Engraving

Personalize your purchase with names, dates, or special messages engraved by our master artisans. Available for brass, wood, and glass surfaces.

Secure, insured transport for fragile antique items. We use custom foam inserts and climate-controlled vehicles for delivery.
